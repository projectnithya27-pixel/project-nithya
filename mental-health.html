Mental Health Support

You don't have to carry this alone.

Whatever you're feeling — grief, anxiety, numbness, exhaustion — it deserves space, not silence.

Write to us ↓

Our approach

Held, not handled.
Heard, not assessed.

"You don't need to perform wellness here. You just need to show up as you are."

Project Nithya's mental health support begins with one simple act: listening. Not to assess, not to categorise, not to fill a form — but to really listen, without a clock on the wall.

We work alongside trained counsellors, peer supporters, and women who have been through their own journeys of healing. Whatever shape your need takes, we will find the right support to match it.

What we offer

Support that meets you
exactly where you are.

Mental health is not a destination you arrive at — it is a practice of returning to yourself, again and again. We create the conditions for that return.

Grief & Loss

Loss doesn't follow a schedule. Whether you're mourning a person, a relationship, a version of yourself, or a dream — we hold space without pressure to "move on" or "be strong."

Anxiety & Burnout

For women carrying the weight of too many roles and too many expectations — we help you breathe, reconnect with yourself, and find solid ground beneath the overwhelm.

Trauma & Recovery

From childhood experiences to recent events — trauma shapes us, but it does not define us. Healing is not linear, and you don't have to walk that path without company.

Relationships & Identity

Navigating difficult relationships, separation, family conflict, identity questions, or life transitions — with a steady, compassionate presence beside you through all of it.

Confidential  ·  Read within 24 hours

Tell us what you're
going through.

You don't need the right words. You don't need to have it figured out. Just write what feels true — we will read every word.

All messages are confidential. We do not share your details with anyone outside our team.

What happens next

A transparent process,
every step of the way.

01

We read your message

Every message is read personally by a trained team member — not a bot, not an auto-response. Within 24 hours.

02

We reach out to you

We'll contact you through your preferred channel for a gentle, no-pressure conversation about what kind of support fits you best.

03

We walk with you

Whether that's peer support, counsellor referral, or simply being a consistent, understanding presence — we go at your pace.
