Health Guidance

Navigate your health with confidence.

The healthcare system can feel opaque and overwhelming. We make it clear, accessible, and less lonely.

How we guide you

Your health.
Your questions.

"No question is too small. No concern is too vague. If your body is worrying you, that already matters."

Women's health questions are too often dismissed with a wave or a hurried explanation. Project Nithya exists to close that gap — offering thoughtful, plain-language guidance from people who take your concerns seriously.

We are not a replacement for medical care. We are the bridge that helps you access it — clearly, confidently, and without the usual overwhelm.

Know your healthcare rights

Right to informed consent

No procedure should happen without your understanding and agreement. You may ask questions and refuse treatment.

Right to a second opinion

You are always entitled to seek another doctor's view — especially before major procedures or surgery.

Right to your medical records

Hospitals must provide you copies of your own records, test results, and discharge summaries on request.

Right to free emergency care

Under Indian law, no public or private hospital can deny emergency treatment due to inability to pay.
