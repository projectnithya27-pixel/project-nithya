Who we are

Two parallel journeys,
one shared path.

Project Nithya was born from a simple truth: no one should navigate the labyrinth of autoimmune illness alone. We walk alongside patients, families, and caregivers — offering clarity where there is confusion, and community where there is isolation.

Our name, Nithya, means eternal in Sanskrit. Like the persistent hope of those who live with chronic conditions, our commitment is unwavering. We believe that with the right guidance, every individual can reclaim their story.

"Between diagnosis and understanding, there is a vast silence. We fill that silence with knowledge, compassion, and an unwavering human presence."

"Where two paths converge, healing begins — in the shared witness of those who truly understand the walk."
